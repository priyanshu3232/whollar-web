The way households will be won from here

Not a lead list, and not another price comparison page. A channel where demand arrives already decided, already grouped, and timed to the month it can actually move.

LANE 01 Win the street

Bid for a group of households whose contracts all come up at once. They have already decided to look. You are competing on your offer, not on who called first.

LANE 02 Defend the ones you have

Some of those households are already yours, and about to leave. Bid to keep them at a cost you set, before they churn. Losing them costs you a replacement. Keeping them costs you a bid.

WHERE THE MONEY COMES FROM Turn retention cost into acquisition

Every dollar in a win-back offer is a dollar spent on a customer you already had. Move that budget to the front, spend it on the rate you bid, and the same money that used to rescue one customer now wins a street.
